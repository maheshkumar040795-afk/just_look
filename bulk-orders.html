Bulk Order Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is typically 30 pieces per style, though this can vary based on fabric and design complexity. Contact us for exact details.

Can you add our school/company logo?

Yes, we offer custom logo embroidery and printing for all bulk institutional orders.

Do you provide size samples before full production?

Yes, we develop and share a sample for approval before starting bulk production, especially for first-time orders.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timelines depend on order size and customization, but we always share an estimated schedule upfront and deliver across Tamil Nadu.

Do you offer fabric choices?

Yes, we offer multiple fabric grades and finishes to match your budget and comfort requirements.
